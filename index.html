About Wisetree Consult

WiseTree Consult Nigeria Limited is a software development and software marketing company providing information technology services, solutions and products to business enterprises in Nigeria & Africa. WiseTree Consult provides software-based solutions to banking, finance, trading and service organizations using appropriate tools such as: Enterprise Resource Planning Software (ERP), Fixed Asset Management Software, Human Resource & Payroll systems, etc. As a corporate member of Computer Professional of Nigeria, Institute of Software Practitioner of Nigeria (ISPON), we have the provision of affordable, reliable and efficient Software solution as our primary objective. We are indeed committed to nurturing information technology to future height with service that is qualitative, fulfilling, affordable, and which can also stand the test of generations to come. .
